Personal Interests and Hobbies

These hobbies reset my mental health and take my mind elsewhere, away from the stressful routine of my daily life.

Chess

Chess is a puzzle and a game, an art and a science all rolled up in one. It is a fun challenge to see if I can outwit an opponent and win the game and it’s also a fun challenge to sit back and retrace my moves to see where I went wrong. I am always thinking the hardest when playing chess and love that there is always more to learn.

Graphic Design

I use graphic design as my artistic outlet. Currently I focus on portraits and figure drawings that capture movement but I love sitting down in front of my tablet to draw anything! It doesn't matter what, because I know I am refining my skills as a result. Similar to coding, it's extremely satisfying to turn any new idea into a new project - whatever idea I get, I can turn it into something.

Surfing

Founding member of the UC Irvine Surf Team!

The ocean is my sanctuary and favorite place to meditate. I love to feel the water on my skin and just sit peacefully while I’m waiting for waves and think about life, or nothing at all.

Climbing

Whether I’m climbing up the side of a mountain or on the rock wall, I’m entirely focused on what I’m doing, without even trying to. When I climb, nothing in the world exists except my body and the surrounding few square meters ahead of me. I also love that the challenges are multifaceted: strength, endurance, technique, intelligence, and courage.
